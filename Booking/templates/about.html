History

Carléns started its operations in Säter, Dalarna, in 1939 and has since then operated several salons in Stockholm.

In 1986, a salon was opened in the Slakthusområdet area near Globen, and in 2006, it was taken over by Pernilla (the 4th generation Carlén) together with Helene. Due to significant changes in the Slakthusområdet area, we were forced to relocate. After 35 years in the same premises, we moved to Nynäsvägen 363, where we currently operate our business.

Employees

Pernilla

Pernilla is the fourth generation at Carléns. She followed in her father's footsteps and achieved remarkable success as a junior hairstylist, winning gold in the Swedish Championship (SM), Nordic Championship (NM), European Championship (EM), and World Championship (VM). After renting a chair at several salons in the city, she became the co-owner of Carlen & Co along with Helene in 2006. Pernilla holds a journeyman's certificate in hairdressing.

Helene

Helene started as a trainee at Carléns in 1987 and worked as an employee for many years. After renting a chair at another salon for a few years, she returned to Carléns in 2006, this time as a co-owner along with Pernilla. She holds a journeyman's certificate in hairdressing.

Agneta

Agneta has previously owned her own salon and has also worked as an employee and rented a chair at several places in Stockholm. Since 2006, she has been renting a chair at Carlen & Co. She holds a journeyman's certificate in hairdressing.
